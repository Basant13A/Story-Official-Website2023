Her Choice is What Decides Her Story

  After being pushed to her death by a mysterious figure, Park Eun-ha reincarnates in the world of a novel as Raeliana McMillan, a side character destined to be poisoned by her fiancé. To change her story she needs a plan... 6 months pretending to be the fake fiancée of the novel's male protagonist, Duke Noah Wynknight. But will partnering with this cold-hearted, angel-faced demon of a man really help her avoid another ill-fated ending or is there more to uncover?!

Characters

'Immerse Yourself in the Unforgettable and Unique Characters of the Story'

Raeliana Mcmilian

A very open and extroverted person. At times she is shown to be sarcastic and dramatic. She is brave, determined and soft to her loved ones especially Rosemary, her little sister.

Noah Wynknight

He is sarcastic, calculative and a clever leader. He focuses on his goals and carrying his title as a duke. He is also kown for having the smile of an angel but...

Adam Taylor

A genius young knight serving under Duke Wynknight, is shown to be a very quiet individual, who only seems to speak to people he trusts. He is also the duke's very close aid.

Heika Demint

He is known for his distant demeanor, stemming from his struggles with social interaction. Despite this, his disciples appreciate his teachings.

Siathrich Chamers

He is the current King of Chamers and known to have a somewhat meddlesome and talkative personality and is known to annoy his brother, Noah.

Naomi O'Brien

She is the assistant of the king Siatrich Wynknight and he relies on her alot in his work.
